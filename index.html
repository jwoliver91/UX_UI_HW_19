    CollaboTravel

    Problem

    Traveling with a group can be overwhelming and frustrating. The group’s primary planner feels burnt out while other group members feel excluded from the decision making process.

    Solution

    CollaboTravel provides one location that houses the itinerary, calendar, and group chat. The voting feature ensures that all members of the group feel included in the process, and provides an equitable decision making process.

    USDA Website Redesign

    Problem

    Users are seeking information about food health and recalls, but struggling to find them on the overwhelming USDA website. Our second user group is looking for resources to make farming easier and more sustainable. Farming is long, hard work. Finding resources shouldn’t be.

    Solution

    By redesigning the site information architecture, and adding additional points of exposure, users are able to easily find important resources like Grants & Loans and Food Recalls.

    Fishbowl

    Problem

    Decision fatigue impacts us everyday. After a long day making choices at work, deciding on a dining option, especially with a group, can feel nearly impossible.

    Solution

    Fishbowl helps to pick dining options for the user to alleviate any stress involved in deciding dinner. With an easy veto process, all users feel empowered and the back-and-forth on “what should we do for dinner?” can become a thing of the past.
